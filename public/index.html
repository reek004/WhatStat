About Us

WhatStat is a cutting-edge web application tailored to analyze exported WhatsApp text files, delivering users comprehensive insights and visualizations derived from their chat data. Our platform transforms raw conversations into engaging statistics, allowing users to understand messaging patterns. With a user-friendly interface, WhatStat facilitates seamless navigation. Moreover, it offers a unique feature to effortlessly share these compelling statistics across diverse social media platforms, enhancing the overall user experience and making chat analysis an interactive and shareable journey.
